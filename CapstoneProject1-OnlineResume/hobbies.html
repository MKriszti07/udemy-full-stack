My Hobbies & Free Time

🏃 Sports & Active Lifestyle

Staying active is an important part of my everyday life. I work out at home and go to the gym on a weekly basis, and I also practice Aikido regularly. Besides structured training, I love going for hikes and walks — it's a great way to clear my head and enjoy nature.

🎭 Entertainment & Leisure

When it comes to unwinding, I enjoy a wide variety of activities. I love going to the cinema and theatre, as well as attending concerts, city events, and festivals. At home, I spend quality time watching films and series, and I'm also an avid reader.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family & Friends

Some of my favourite moments are the ones spent with family and friends. Whether it's a cosy get-together with homemade food, a BBQ, a day trip, or celebrating special occasions together — I truly value these shared experiences. Travelling with the people I love is also something I hold close to my heart.

📚 Self-Development

I believe in continuously growing, both personally and professionally. I'm always looking to expand my programming knowledge, and I also enjoy learning new languages in my spare time.
